Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What chemicals are used by Mosquito Man?

A: We use Bifenthrin and cedar oil, however we offer an organic package which uses garlic and cedar oil.


Q: How long should we wait to enter our yard after a treatment?

A: The label states 30 minutes before re-entering your yard after treatment, however we recommend one hour to be on the safe side.


Q: Does Mosquito Man offer services for other pests and insects?

A: Unfortunately Morsquito Man only offers services for mosquitos and ticks.


Q: How often does Mosquito Man treat your yard?

A: We treat yards every two weeks; however we also offer a one treatment party package perfect for barbeques and birthday parties!


Q: How long does the treatment last?

A: The treatment lasts up to 21 days. We treat every two weeks to make sure you have constant coverage throughout the summer.


Q: Will the treatment wash away during a rainstorm?

A: No, even with heavy rain the treatment should still last up to 21 days.


Q: Are your prices listed on the website?

A: Yes! Check out our services page for our price list and treatment options.


Q: If I am unhappy with the service am I entitled to a refund?

A: We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee policy. If you are unhappy with the service we fully refund your money.


Q: What if I am only unhappy with the recent treatment?

A: Anytime a customer is unhappy they can request a service call which is provided free of charge.


Q: Are you locked into a contract when signing up for service?

A: There are no contracts, customers can cancel at any time. At the end of the season customers also can request to receive treatment the following season or to stop services.


Q: Does Mosquito Man treat my yard based on a scheduled day and time?

A: Unfortunately due to weather and service calls we are unable to guarantee a set time and day of treatment. We can guarantee that our service will always be within the two week timeframe.


Q: If I have a dog how will I know when to leave them in the house for a treatment day?

A: We offer call ahead service upon request. We will provide the courtesy of a phone call the night before a scheduled treatment so you can prepare for service.
